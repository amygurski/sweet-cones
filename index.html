About Sweet Cones

Welcome to Sweet Cones — San Francisco's favourite ice cream truck! We roll through the city serving hand-scooped, small-batch ice cream made with the finest local ingredients. Run by Dash and Gabe, we're here to make every stop feel like a celebration with flavours everyone can enjoy.

Our slogan says it all: Melt into Happiness. Stop by, grab a cone, and let the magic begin.
