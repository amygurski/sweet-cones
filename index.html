About Sweet Cones

Welcome to Sweet Cones — San Francisco's favourite ice cream truck! We roll through the city serving hand-scooped, small-batch ice cream made with the finest local ingredients. Whether you're craving a classic vanilla cone or an adventurous lavender honey scoop, we've got something to make you smile.

Our slogan says it all: Melt into Happiness. Stop by, grab a cone, and let the magic begin.
